Use the sprint_status tool to read the sprint state.

Produce a sprint retrospective:

## Summary
- Sprint name and goal
- Completed: N/M items (X%)
- Duration: start → end

## What Shipped
- List completed items with assignee and time taken

## What Didn't Ship
- List incomplete items with reasons

## Blockers Encountered
- List blockers and how they were (or weren't) resolved

## Velocity
- Items completed per day
- Average time per item

## Recommendations
- What to do differently next sprint
